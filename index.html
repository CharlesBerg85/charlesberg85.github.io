Who I am

Game & Web Developer based out of California

Hello! I am a self-taught game developer based in Southern California with a certification in Full Stack Development. My passion for creating games and passion for coding led me to pursue a career in the tech industry, and I am constantly striving to improve my skills and knowledge.

Before becoming a computer programmer, I owned and operated my own business, Independent Mobility Inc., for about 9 years. During that time, I managed all aspects of the company, including employee workloads, project management, website design, customer relations, and more. Running my own business gave me valuable experience in leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving.

I am proud to have co-developed the TC Connect application, which was my final project at Bethel School of Technology. TC Connect is a social networking application that helps Teen Challenge Alumni stay in touch after graduation, and was developed using Angular and Firebase. This project was the culmination of my studies at Bethel and was a great opportunity to apply the skills I had learned in a real-world setting.

In addition to TC Connect, I have worked on a number of other projects, including Bacon Bastion, a simple 2D one player dungeon crawler. I am also working on an ecommerce store using .Net Blazor Client Side, as well as an untitled 4-player role-playing game. Furthermore, I created an application for a video game collector using C# and Angular, which allows users to keep track of their owned games and fill in information for each game using an API.

Recently, I worked at GravityJack as a game developer, where I was involved in the development of a VR online multiplayer social deduction game, platformed in the Metaverse. During my time at GravityJack, I honed my skills in source control using GitHub Desktop, debugging, and task prioritization. I also used professional design tools to create characters, levels, layouts, and other unique elements for the game.

I am married to my amazing wife Courtney for 10 years, and together we have three beautiful daughters, Eisley, Elfine, and Esther. We are also expecting another child in August. My faith is an important part of my life and guides me in everything I do. In my free time, I enjoy gaming, politics, reading, and working out. I also stream on Twitch under the name Offended_Lettuce.

In terms of my technical skills, I specialize in Unity Engine, C#, and the .NET Frameworks. I have a strong understanding of software development principles and am always looking for opportunities to improve my skills and knowledge. If you would like to learn more about my work or discuss potential projects, please feel free to reach out to me via email or phone.
